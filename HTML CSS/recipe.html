Chicken Adobo

Good day! This webpage is dedicated to teach you about the no.1 dish of The Philippines. The Chicken Adobo is a savory chicken dish with soy sauce and vinegar. It is very delicious and comes with a lot of twist in my country. Here's my own recipe for you to follow, I hope you find this recipe delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1 kilogram of chicken
  • 3 pcs of dried bay leaves
  • 6 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 7 tablespoon of white vinegar
  • 8 cloves of garlic (the more garlic, the more it will taste good)
  • 1 1/2 cup of water
  • 3 tablespoon of cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon of brown sugar
  • 2 pcs of chili (optional)
  • ground pepper

Cooking Procedure

  1. Marinate the chicken in the soy sauce and garlic for 1 hour or overnight.
  2. Separate the chicken form the garlic to avoid burning the garlic.
  3. Pan fry the chicken to get a golden brown crust.
  4. Pour the marinade in the chicken after you get the crust you want. You can now add the pepper and chili here.
  5. Simmer for 20-30 minutes before adding the vinegar and let it cook for 10 minutes.
  6. Add sugar to balance the little bit of the acidity, You can also add additional salt to taste.
